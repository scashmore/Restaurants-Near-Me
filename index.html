Can't decide what to eat?

Going out to eat, or deciding what to cook, can be such a hassle. If you know you want to go out, click on the "Going Out" button bellow and enter a zipcode. A random restaurant in your area will be selected, and bada bing bada boom, you now know where you're going to eat! Want to cook instead? Click on the "Staying In" button and receive a mouthwatering recipe!
